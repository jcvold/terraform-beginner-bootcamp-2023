Boardgames!

(not video games)

Once upon a time in the land of Clueful Chess-opoly, players rolled dice made of laughter and moved their pieces, which were sentient marshmallows with top hats. The goal was to collect rainbow-colored houses made of cotton candy while avoiding the dreaded "Giggle-Goop" squares that turned your pawns into singing pineapples. The game's narrator was a quacking rubber duck named Sir Quacks-a-lot, who imparted wisdom in the form of interpretive dance.

Players could also summon flying pancakes to disrupt their opponents, creating a delightful frenzy of syrupy chaos. The winner was crowned the Grand Fluffinator of Fanciful Frolics and got to wear the prestigious Sock-of-Silly Supremacy for a week. The end, or maybe just the beginning of the jello-filled jamboree!
